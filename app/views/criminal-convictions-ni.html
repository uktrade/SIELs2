Disclose criminal convictions

Has anyone relating to the application been convicted of either:

  • a criminal offence resulting in 4 years or more in prison?
  • an offence under part of the European Arrest Warrant?

This includes you, anyone in your organisation, or anyone who has powers of representation, decision or control.

Which part of the European Arrest Warrant?
Specifically, an offence within the meaning of Article 2(2) of Council Framework Decision 2002/584/JHA of 13 June 2002 of the European Arrest Warrant and the surrender procedures between Member States (as amended by Council Framework Decision 2009/299/JHA of 26 February 2009).
